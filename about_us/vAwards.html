Victor Awards


In memory of our Founding Director, Victor Podagrosi, an annual award has been established to honor individuals and organizations who have demonstrated outstanding commitment, generosity, and/or service to Child's Play Touring Theatre and the arts community.

Each year, at the International Showcase of Performing Arts for Young People, Child's Play awards an International Victor Award to children's theatre companies who show excellence in their ability to interact with sponsors in a prompt, efficient and compatible manner, and who demonstrate a positive attitude toward their art.
