Child's Play Summer Camp

Child’s Play camps provide children with the opportunity to create original stories and poems, adapt them from page to stage, and perform an original production for family/friends!

Encouraging skills in ensemble building, self-esteem, team building, and problem solving, Child’s Play teaching artists inspire campers by providing instruction in writing, acting, improvisation, juggling, movement, dance, and music.

Be the playwright and the star! From writing to rehearsal to performance, Child’s Play's drama camp is unique experience that encourages ensemble building, self-esteem, artistic thinking and problem solving. Campers develop skills in acting, improvisation, signing, music, dancing, writing, state direction and more. A high artist-to student ration ensures a fun, safe and supportive camp.

Child's Play Touring Theatre
Summer Camp at the Goodman Theatre in Chicago, Illinois!


This is a dress rehearsal for Childs Play Touring Theatre in a past Summer Theatre camp at the Goodman Theatre. Kid's Put it on the Page AND Put it on the Stage! Child's Play camps are for children 6-13 and provide children with the opportunity to create original stories and poems, adapt them from page to stage, and perform an original production for family and friends! Encouraging skills in ensemble building, self-esteem, team building, and problem solving, Child's Play teaching artists inspire campers by providing instruction in writing, acting, improvisation, juggling, movement, dance, and music.

Over the course of the camp, young performers are immersed into a world of theatrical creation, offering them a safe environment in which to share their talents, discover new talents, and broaden their knowledge of the creative process. Special guest artists from acclaimed program Mummies & Masterpieces Joined us in our creative camp process! As specialists in visual arts, teaching artists from Mummies & Masterpieces they introduced campers to famous sculptures, paintings, and much much more as a means to inspire students in their creative writing process.
