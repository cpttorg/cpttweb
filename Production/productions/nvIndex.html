World Premieres

Your organization can elect to have our World Premiere option by presenting a "New Voices" production.  For this special project, the company will select stories, poems and essays written by children in your school or community to rehearse and then perform to the young authors and their peers.  The show is produced with full costumes, props and sets.  An exciting award ceremony is held for the talented young writers who receive a Certificate of Creativity for their work.

The opportunity for young writers to see their words transformed into live theatre by professional actors is one of the most exciting aspects of Child's Play. To kick-off your "New Voices" program, you may consider having a Writing Is... Child's Play workshop several months before your performance as a way to enhance the writing experience.
