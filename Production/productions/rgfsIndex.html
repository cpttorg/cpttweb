Really Good and Funny Show

Child's Play brings you our favorite songs, plays and dances from our vast repertory of stories and poems written by children over the last three decades. This delightful audience participation show will carry you along from curtain up to curtain call. It's our best of the best . . . so let Child's Play take you for a ride! A great show for summer.
