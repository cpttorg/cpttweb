Animal Tales and Dinosaur Scales

Geared to younger audience's interest and fascination with animal characters, this presentation gives the little ones plenty of opportunities to sing and play along with the pros. Stories include a cautionary fable about a lion and a cloud, the story of a tap dancing dinosaur and an entertaining, interactive song about mammals of the sea.
