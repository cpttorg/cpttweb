Kids for President

Child’s Play Touring Theatre of Chicago presents a show timed to coincide with children’s election year curiosity and excitement. “Kids for President” runs the gamut from silly to serious with campaign speeches, suggestions for improving the world, and kids’ own ideas on what it’s like to be the President, this show will win the hearts and votes of both children and adults!Join the campaign trail and bring "Kids for President" to your community or school.
